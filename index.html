Stars and Scribbles

Description:

A generative interactive web application that allows you to select different qualities of star shapes and circles, as well as customize the background color and star qualities using the interface on the right.

Parameters:

  • circles: number of circles on screen
    r: amount of red in the background
    g: amount of green in the background
    b: amount of blue in the background
    stars: number of stars on screen
    points: umber of points on the stars
    radius1: inside radius of stars
    radius2: outside radius of stars
